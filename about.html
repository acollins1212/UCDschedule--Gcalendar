About

I got the idea for this when I was putting my schedule into my phone calendar. I realized that I was able to write a program that could do this for me.

Hopefully this saves a lot of people time from tedious schedule making!

You can export a Google calendar to your calendar on iPhone, Android, etc. Look up "how to import ics files" for your device
